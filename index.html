Welcome to Aarhus

Hello and welcome to Aarhus, the second biggest city in Denmark and home to over 350,000. We realized that moving here is stressful enough, letalone finding quiet locations to relax in your own time, that's where this site comes in! We plan to regularly update this page with new and varied hidden locations throughout the city and easily accessible by bus. Please click one of the links on the side of the page to read about the location!

Marselisborg Slotspark

Nestled between beautiful forests and the coastline lies Marselisborg Slot, summer home of the Danish royal family, however did you know the gardens are open to the public most of the year, and contain beautiful sculptures, ponds, and plants, as well as great views of the city.

Cafe Mellemfolk

Café Mellemfolk in Mejlgade 53 is not just any café - they are a café with attitudes! Run by volunteers, they set high standards for our products, and then they donate their profits to projects that work for a more sustainable and fair world around the globe. In Café Mellemfolk you can drink a cup of organic FairTrade coffee at good prices. You can enjoy one of several delicious organic beers; choose from everything from lagers to IPAs and sours. Or you can eat a delicious vegan panini with homemade bread. They have something for everyone - and they have plenty of space for both you and your friends!
